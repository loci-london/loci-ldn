about this map

ge·nius loci [ˌdʒiːniəs ˈləʊkʌɪ, ˌdʒiːniəs ˈləʊsʌɪ, ˌdʒiːniəs ˈləʊki] noun  • the prevailing character or atmosphere of a place.  • the presiding god or spirit of a place. what is the essence of london? what do our memories tell us? and are the tunes that are laced into them a coincidence? or are we building a soundtrack to our metropolis? LOCI:LDN is a digital archive to preserve your memory of music in a specific location within landaan town. each submission is completely anonymous. we wanna hear your raw stories. are we talking compass-based postcodes, boroughs or zones? a station, cafe, bridge or someone’s back garden? whether it’s where you spent your uni lunches or a concert that stuck with you — a song you shazamed at a record shop or the ep you played on repeat during that one especially rainy autumn — leave your mark on the map.
